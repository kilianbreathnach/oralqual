Goals of the Project

  • Cosmology
    • Determine useful things about cosmological models from redshift survey data
    • Improve constraints on current cosmological parameters with new data and techniques
  • Dark Matter
    • Model Dark Matter better
    • Test alternative theories of the dark matter content of the universe
